This is a living document that I will continue to update over time. It's simply a place for me to store all of my favorite tools and apps that I consistently use.

### My Own Apps
- [Gifted](https://getgifted.app). This is a gift-tracking and wishlist app that I built. It is still a work in progress, but most of the main functionality is there. Feel free to sign up for an account and try it out! It is mobile only, and just a web-app at the moment, but I have plans for a desktop version, and native mobile and desktop apps are coming in the future.
- [Limitlist](https://www.limitlist.app). This is a list-making app I am working on. Stay tuned!

### Financial

- [YNAB](https://www.youneedabudget.com/). This is the only budgeting app one could ever need. It has given me complete financial peace of mind.

### Productivity/Lifestyle
- [TickTick](https://ticktick.com). I have tried out almost every major todo app you can imagine. And I don't really love any of them. However, TickTick is the best that I've found, and I use it religiously. My main gripe with it is how it looks, if it was prettier looking and had better navigation, I think it would be perfect.
- [Strides](https://www.stridesapp.com/). This is my favorite habit tracker, it's just so well designed and easy to use.
- [Bear](https://bear.app/). This is what I use for my notes. Bear was really awesome when it first came out, but I've found that the product has become rather stale. I wish it had the ability to create multiple "notebooks", and it has some occasional bugs and weird quirks, but overall, it's still the best notes app I have used. 

### Health & fitness
- [Strong](https://www.strong.app/). This app has really come a long way since when I first used it. The UI is minimal and clean, and the Apple Watch integration is just great.
- [MyFitnessPal](https://www.myfitnesspal.com/). This one is a given, I'm sure you've heard of it. Although it's not perfect, it works. I just wish it wasn't so expensive for premium, but during the times I am bulking, it is worth it.
